Ingredients

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour, sifted
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 2 tbsp White Sugar
    • Man this has a lot of things
  • 3/4 cup plus 2 tablespoon milk
  • 2 tbsp Unsalted Butter, melted
  • 1 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
  • Canola oil

Steps

Time for the nitty gritty

  1. Combine flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder into a bowl and mix well.

  2. In a separate bowl, mix the egg, milk, vanilla, and melted butter together.

    3. Be careful not to use hot, melted butter as that may end up cooking your eggs!

  3. Pour the wet ingrediente mixture into the dry ingredients and stir mostly till mostly incorporated.

    The batter should be thick and a little bumpy, but without any visible streaks of flour.

    If it is too thick to work with, add a little milk. Set the batter aside for 5-10 minutes.

  4. Heat a heavy-bottomed, non-stick pan over a low-medium heat and coat it with oil.

    Use a 1/4 cup measure to scoop and drop the batter into the pan so that you get evenly-sized pancakes

  5. Cook until first side is golden brown, or until the top surface forms bubbles. Flip and repeat.

  6. Enjoy!
