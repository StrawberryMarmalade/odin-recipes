Pumpkin Pie Recipe

In this essay I will speak about the pumpkin pie recipe experience---

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 can pumpkin puree
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 unbaked 9-inch deep-dish pie shell

Steps

  1. Mix sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger and cloves in a small bowl. Beat eggs in large bowl.

    Stir in pumpkin and sugar-spice mixture. Gradually stir in evaporated milk.

  2. Pour into pie shell.
  3. Bake in preheated 425F oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350F.

    Bake for 40 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.

    Cool on wire rack for 2 hours. Serve immediately or refrigerate.

    Enjoy!
